Ölstugan Tullen Lejonet

Nestled in the heart of Göteborg at Friggagatan 27, Ölstugan Tullen Bar beckons patrons with its warm ambiance and rich history. This beloved establishment is a sanctuary for beer enthusiasts and culinary adventurers alike, offering a carefully curated selection of locally brewed beers that reflect the essence of Swedish brewing traditions. The inviting atmosphere, coupled with a commitment to exceptional service, makes Ölstugan Tullen Bar on Friggagatan 27 a go-to destination for those seeking an authentic taste of Swedish hospitality. Whether you're unwinding with friends or savoring the diverse flavors of Swedish cuisine, this Ölstugan Tullen Bar promises a memorable and delightful experience in the heart of Göteborg.
